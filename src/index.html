Rules

Game Play

  1. Must have 2 players
  2. 3 rounds - 3 rounds make up 1 game
  3. Each player starts with a score of 0
  4. Turns end when one person guesses incorrectly to the current survey
  5. Each player's score is displayed by their name
  6. The winner is determined by the player with the highest total score at the end of 3 rounds

Round

  1. In the first two rounds, players will take turns guessing responses to the survey.
  2. The round is over when all responses to the survey have been guessed
  3. The third round is a "Fast Money" round: each player gets 30 seconds to enter as many responses as they can think of.

Survey

  • A single, random survey is chosen at the start of every round
  • The survey chosen at the start of each round should not be shown more than once in a game
  • Each survey includes a question and the top 3 responses
  • Each response has a number associated with it that represents the number of people who gave that response
  • Initially, only the survey question is displayed and all responses are hidden.
  • As a player guesses a response, the survey should check its responses for a match
  • If there is a match, that answer should be revealed along with the number of respondents who gave it
  • If there is no match, the players turn will swtich
  • Responses should be displayed on the board in the order of most popular to least popular
